Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website or use our mobile application. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our Service.

How We Use Cookies

Sweats uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, provide our services, and understand how our platform is being used.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your login session Session
security_token Protects against security threats Session
preferences Remembers your app settings 1 year

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website and app by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration

Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
ad_tracking Enables targeted advertising 90 days
campaign_source Tracks marketing campaign effectiveness 30 days

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services that appear on our pages. We use reputable third parties for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics to understand user behavior
  • Customer Support: Help desk and chat services
  • Social Media: Social sharing and login integration
  • Advertising: Relevant ad delivery and measurement

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your browser
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Allow cookies only from specific sites
  • Get notified when a cookie is being set

Mobile App Settings

In our mobile app, you can manage data collection preferences through:

  • App Settings > Privacy > Data Collection
  • Account Settings > Privacy Preferences
  • Device Settings > App Permissions

Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of certain third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Advertising: Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Settings

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our Service may not function properly:

  • You may need to log in each time you visit
  • Your preferences and settings may not be saved
  • Some social and sharing features may not work
  • We may not be able to provide personalized experiences

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and app.

Mobile-Specific Data Collection

In our mobile app, we may collect additional information through:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique identifiers for your device
  • App Usage Data: How you interact with app features
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you relevant notifications
  • Crash Reports: To improve app stability and performance
