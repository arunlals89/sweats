Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan at any time?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences.
Is there a free trial for Premium?
Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial for Premium so you can experience all the advanced features risk-free.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your billing period.
